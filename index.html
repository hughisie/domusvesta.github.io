Welcome to DomusVesta

Welcome to DomusVesta – the UK's people-powered digital marketplace for trusted home services. Whether you need a mobile doctor, a skilled gardener, a reliable painter, a hairdresser, a yoga instructor, or a legal advisor – we bring care and craft into every home.

At DomusVesta, we're not just building a platform. We're building trust, opportunity, and community. With verified local professionals, secure bookings, and end-to-end support, we make it easy to find quality services with peace of mind.

Our mission is simple but powerful: To empower households and professionals alike through smart technology, meaningful human connection, and a shared commitment to excellence.

Why DomusVesta?

The Market Gap We're Bridging

Every year, thousands of UK households struggle with:

  • Unverified service providers and fake reviews
  • No-shows, unclear pricing, and no support when things go wrong
  • A lack of access to skilled professionals across essential trades and wellbeing services

Meanwhile, thousands of skilled professionals and small businesses face:

  • Inconsistent income and platform fees that punish growth
  • Lack of visibility or trust signals to win new clients
  • No structured way to build careers, upskill, or train the next generation

DomusVesta exists to fix that.

We combine technology and heart to offer a safer, smarter, and more human way to connect. A platform where professionals are valued, independent traders are supported, and customers are protected.

We're here to level the playing field for independent service providers and connect communities through meaningful, quality-first work.
