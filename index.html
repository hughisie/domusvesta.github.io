Our Mission

Creating impact beyond service

To connect UK households with trustworthy, verified local service professionals via a secure, intuitive platform—while actively supporting skilling, reskilling, and inclusive growth.

Our Vision

To be the UK's most reliable and people-centric home services platform, while empowering communities to thrive through local economic activation and skills development.

Through partnerships with colleges and training institutions, DomusVesta will help individuals skill and reskill, ensuring the services offered reflect the evolving needs of local areas—addressing shortages and creating meaningful economic opportunities.
